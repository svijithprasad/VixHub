Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba

Original title: Kimetsu no Yaiba

TV Series 2019 to still airing 

Action|Adventure|Comedy|Drama|Shounen|Death of a Loved One|Demons|Historical|Martial Arts|Orphans|Siblings|Supernatural|Swordplay

A family is attacked by demons and only two members survive - Tanjiro and his sister Nezuko, who is turning into a demon slowly. Tanjiro sets out to become a demon slayer to avenge his family and cure his sister. Bloodthirsty demons lurk in the woods, and nobody dares to venture out at night, save for the demon slayer of legend. Surviving in this harsh world, young Tanjirou takes it upon himself to protect his family until the day that everything is taken from him in a vicious slaughter. Now, all he has left is his sister, and she's not even human anymore.


Creator: Koyoharu Gotouge


Stars: Natsuki Hanae|Zach Aguilar|Abby Trott
